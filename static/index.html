Step-by-Step Dashboard Process (Easy Guide)

1

Start the chatbot & configure targets

Go to **"New Analysis"**. The AI assistant chatbot will guide you. Input your organization name, industry, and employee size. Describe your specific pain points (e.g. "inventory costs, slow conversion, low ROI") in plain English so the system knows what metrics to look for.

2

Load your spreadsheet

Drag and drop your metrics CSV file directly into the chat bubble workspace. If you just want to explore the features, simply click **"Use Demo Dataset"** to load pre-seeded corporate ledger metrics instantly.

3

Interact with the Dashboard & simulate scenario multipliers

Your dashboard renders historical trends, z-score anomalies, and a 6-month ARIMA forecast. Use the **What-If Simulator** sliders (Marketing, R&D, Sales) to model strategic changes and instantly watch the predicted revenue and ROI projections adjust!

4

Customize, Edit, & Download your Report

Click **"Edit Insights"** to modify KPIs, SWOT matrix bullets, or strategic recommendations manually. When done, click **"Save Changes"** to write modifications to SQLite, choose your company color theme using the color selector, and click **"Download PDF Report"** to export a formatted corporate growth audit.
